Nothing in cybersecurity ever stays the same. To take your career to the next level, you must continually challenge yourself and keep learning.

ISC2 is committed to delivering best-in-class professional development resources that will not only advance your cybersecurity career but also support your ISC2 Certified in Cybersecurity (CC) certification and maintenance goals.

If you haven’t already made a start, explore the continuing education and learning opportunities available to you to earn CPE credits. Submitting learning credits for these activities is handled by us — simply register with the email address in your ISC2 account.

Skill-Builders
2-4 CPE credits
Stay in front of the hottest topics and trends impacting your current role and your cybersecurity career with ISC2 Skill-Builders. Created by industry experts and members like you, they are relevant, easy to consume and available on demand. Free to you as member. Log in and find out more.

News and Insights
1-5 CPE credits
Visit this content hub to find out about the latest threats, policy changes and more, plus access the latest quiz and earn CPE credits. Start reading and learning.

Certificates
4-12 CPE credits
Gain expertise in emerging cybersecurity topics, set yourself on a path to your next certification and earn digital badges to demonstrate your knowledge with ISC2 Certificates. Find out more and access member-exclusive pricing.

Official ISC2 Certification Training
40 CPE credits
Continue strengthening the foundations of your career and work toward defining the core cybersecurity areas and roles that most appeal to you. Explore your training options as you get ready for your next certification exam. As a member, you get 20% off all Official ISC2 Online Certification Readiness.

Events
Various
ISC2 events offer you the opportunity to stay in front of emerging trends in cybersecurity and learn from leading minds in the field. In-person and virtual attendance options allow you to select the event options that work best for you, including Security Congress where you can earn up to 98 CPE credits when you attend in person. Find out more.

As a CC credential holder, you will need to earn 45 CPE credits during your three-year certification cycle to maintain the certification. We recommend earning 15 credits per year.

For a full list of learning activities that earn CPE credits, see page 14 of the ISC2 CPE Handbook.

If you have any questions about your ISC2 membership or certification maintenance requirements, please email membersupport@isc2.org or call us on any of our customer service lines.
