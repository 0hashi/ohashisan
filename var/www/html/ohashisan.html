The Business Continuity, Disaster Recovery and Incident Response concepts provided here are the policies, processes, procedures and controls needed to protect the availability of information and systems, and to restore Ohashisan to normal operation in the event of a serious incident or catastrophic event.

Scope

The business, maintenance, and response activities in this document primarily cover the ohashisan.com web site, the primary and backup high availability servers and operating systems that host ohashisan.com, and the reddirt.lab infrastructure.
