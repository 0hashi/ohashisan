The Business Continuity, Incident Response, and Disaster Recovery concepts provided here are the policies, processes, procedures and controls needed to protect the availability of information and systems for Ohashisan and to restore normal operation in the event of a serious incident or catastrophic event.

Scope

The business, maintenance, and response activities in this document primarily cover the information and systems for the www.ohashisan.com web site, the primary and backup HA servers and operating systems that host www.ohashisan.com, and the reddirt.lab information, systems, and infrastructure.

  1. Business Continuity Response for unexpected changes in operating conditions to keep business operational.
  2. Incident Response Enables business to continue operating throughout a crisis.
  3. Disaster Recovery Activate if BC and IR plans fail to return business to normal operations ASAP.
