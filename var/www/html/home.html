Reddirt.lab is my demo/test environment built on VMWare ESXi, running Windows and Linux Servers and a few bare metal systems, a couple Cisco routers, a NETGEAR switch and a mindless HUB or two. Of course there's always at least one laptop connected via wire or WiFi, plus the usual suspect cellphones and all of the obvious IoT devices that most homes have nowadays.
